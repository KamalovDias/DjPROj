Restaurant complex

The hotel offers all guests, as well as all gourmets of Shymkent, an amazingly diverse selection of high-quality food and drinks in the establishments opened at the hotel. 2 restaurants, cafes and bars are always ready to provide guests with an exceptional level of service.

The flagship project of the hotel is the Kazakhasia restaurant, which was created by the famous design bureau from Japan. In the restaurant, guests are offered to taste the most famous dishes of national cuisine performed by world-class chefs.

Olivia Italian restaurant welcomes fans of the Apennine Peninsula cuisine with traditional Italian dishes prepared by an Italian chef with experience in Michelin-starred restaurants.

White Horse English Bar is an ideal place for connoisseurs of elite drinks. The bar offers the largest selection of whiskey and other drinks in the city. A huge selection of cocktails and live music on weekends round off the look of a place worthy of your attention.

The Atrium Bar is open for those who want to relax in a relaxed atmosphere accompanied by a live piano. Classic interior, comfortable furniture, pleasant music and a glass of something warming - everyone who shares this concept of relaxation will certainly become a regular guest here.

SPA center

The snow room, Turkish hammam, sauna, swimming pool, various types of massages, treatments and treatments in the beauty center give a very special experience to everyone who has been here at least once.

There is also the Vita Bar, where you can relax after or in between treatments with vitamin cocktails or fresh juices.

Conference halls

Rixos halls are ideal for holding events in Shymkent. The hotel's conference and meeting rooms are designed for a variety of events from board meetings to banquets. The 4 fully equipped rooms can accommodate up to 600 people and the professional banquet staff will provide excellent service. At your service are fully equipped rooms with a stage, modern lighting and sound equipment.

Rixos Khadisha Shymkent is more than just a hotel, it is a cultural center. After its opening, a lot will change, because now the level of service in Shymkent has received a new benchmark.
